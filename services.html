Services

What we do

Geophysics

We provide advanced geophysical services to support subsurface imaging, interpretation, and uncertainty reduction across the full exploration, development, and production lifecycle. Our expertise includes seismic interpretation, quantitative analysis, seismic inversion, velocity modelling, and seismic processing oversight. We integrate geophysical data with geological and well information, leveraging AI and machine learning techniques to enhance facies and pattern recognition, attribute analysis, and predictive insights. This enables the delivery of robust structural and stratigraphic frameworks, improved prospect identification, and more confident well planning, field development and infill well decisions.

Reservoir Studies

We deliver integrated reservoir studies to evaluate reservoir presence, quality, and performance in support of exploration and development objectives. Our services include reservoir characterization, volumetric assessment, uncertainty and risk analysis, and support for static and dynamic modelling. By combining geoscience, petrophysical, and engineering inputs—enhanced through AI and machine learning applications—we provide deeper insights into reservoir behaviour, optimise development strategies, and support data-driven decision-making throughout the asset lifecycle.
