About Us

Who we are

SynAtar is a UK-based geophysics and geoscience consultancy focused on reservoir characterisation, seismic interpretation, and integrated subsurface studies. Our team supports exploration and development projects with practical insight and robust technical delivery.

We help clients reduce uncertainty, optimise subsurface models, and turn data into confident decisions.

Services

What we do

Reservoir Studies

Our reservoir studies combine geological, petrophysical, and geophysical data to build reliable reservoir models. We focus on reservoir architecture, continuity, and fluid distribution to support exploration, appraisal, and field development.

Geophysics

SynAtar delivers geophysical interpretation and integration services including seismic interpretation, attribute analysis, and subsurface mapping. We translate seismic data into actionable geological models for better subsurface understanding.
