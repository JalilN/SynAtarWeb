Who we are

Synatar is a UK-based geoscience consultancy providing integrated subsurface solutions to the energy sector. We combine deep technical expertise with data-driven analysis to support exploration, field development, and energy transition initiatives.

With experience across diverse global basins and project lifecycles, Synatar delivers high-quality geophysical and geological insights tailored to client objectives. Our multidisciplinary approach integrates geoscience, engineering, and commercial perspectives to reduce uncertainty, enhance decision-making, and create sustainable subsurface value.
