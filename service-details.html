Service Details

Geophysics & Quantitative Interpretation

Seismic interpretation, quantitative analysis, inversion, rock physics, velocity modelling and processing oversight integrated with geological and well data to improve subsurface definition and decision confidence.

Reservoir Characterisation & Modelling

Integrated geological, petrophysical and engineering studies covering reservoir characterisation, volumetrics, uncertainty analysis, resource evaluation and support for static and dynamic modelling.

Energy Transition & Storage

Subsurface screening, characterisation and risk evaluation for carbon storage, geothermal resources and other emerging energy applications, with a focus on containment, performance and long-term value.

Technical Assurance & Advisory

Independent peer reviews, technical assurance, due diligence and expert advisory support to test assumptions, identify uncertainty and strengthen exploration, development and investment decisions.

Exploration & Prospect Generation

Basin and play analysis, seismic interpretation, petroleum systems evaluation, prospect mapping, volumetric assessment and risking to build robust exploration portfolios and identify high-value opportunities.

Reservoir & Resource Evaluation

Integrated geological, geophysical and petrophysical evaluation to define reservoir distribution, estimate in-place and recoverable resources, quantify uncertainty and support commercial decisions.

Technical Risk Assessments

Independent assessment of geological, geophysical, reservoir and operational uncertainties, with clear risk framing and practical recommendations for project teams and decision-makers.

Support Drilling Operations

Pre-drill prognosis, well-position support, geosteering input, real-time data interpretation and post-well evaluation to improve operational decisions and capture subsurface learning.
